Design Careers Club

About DCC

The UC Davis Design Careers Club was founded in 2016 by Tiffany Trieu after two years of leading successful student-run career fairs for the UCD Design Department. We're a multi-disciplinary club that seeks to unite people from varied background in the pursuit of design. Hosting both large and small events, we work to connect current students with alumni, design professionals, and resources on and off campus.

Mission

The UC Davis Design Career Club aims to empower students to take bold risks not only in their designs, but in their career aspirations.
